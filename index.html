Welcome

I'm a PostDoc at the University of Geneva who studies topics in Geometric Group Theory and Formal Language Theory. For more details on my research topics, please see my Research page.

I am an organiser for the Switzerland Geometry, Algebra and Topology Seminar in which we have bi-weekly talks from young researchers in Switzerland; I am also an organiser for the World of GroupCraft conferences, in particular, I was an organiser for GroupCraft I (2021), GroupCraft II (2022) and GroupCraft III (2023) (also, I plan on being an organiser for any future GroupCrafts).

I manage the YouTube channels for the World of GroupCraft confererences, and for the Switzerland Geometry, Algebra and Topology Seminar (click the link to be taken to the channels). Please, consider subscribing to these YouTube channels if you're interested in the talks. (Almost all talks are uploaded to these channels shortly after they're recorded).

You can find my contact information at the bottom of this page. If you have any questions, corrections or suggestions feel free to email me.
